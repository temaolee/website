At This Moment

Period: 2023, 7/22-8/6 12:00-20:30
Venus: The Eleventh Warehouse, Hualien Cultural & Creative Industries Park
Artists:
TAI Body Theatre, WANG Yu-Ting, LEE Ming-Chieh, Baï LEE, LEE Jo-Mei, LEE Te-Mao, HSIN Pei-Chin, CHIU Chen-Hung, MA Li-Chun, Iris Chun-Tzu Chang, Ohau Chen, NI Xiang, CHEN Yu-Jung, CHEN Chen, Tamás SZVET, HUANG Chia, TENG Po-Jen, CHENG Hsien-Yu

"At This Moment" is a mobile artistic action. In the contemporary era when acceleration has become the norm, this action has invited four forums and more than a dozen artists' works. It is just like the construction of an open infrastructure facility, so that the works can be freely expressed. There are no complicated discussions and no wooden partitions. In the large warehouse, the rhythm of quickly setting the exhibition allows the works to have a direct dialogue and collision with the space, responding to the acceleration with a kind of appearance at this moment.
